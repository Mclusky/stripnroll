about us

Dom Smith

Guitar/Vocals

Son of British immigrants, Dom grew up in Sao Paulo Brazil, and became interested in music at an early age, connecting to the 60’s rock n roll movement with the Stones and Beatles as main influences. After finishing high school, he moved up to Salvador Bahia Brazil where he began studying music at UFBa University (composition and conducting) but never finished the course, getting frustrated with the academic world. Along with all his music activities, he also started a musical equipment hire business and worked in many recording studios with famous artists, recording, playing and touring around the word for several years as a sound engineer. He moved to London around 1991, fulfilling a childhood dream, where he founded and played in many groups including The Moles, Flicker Noise and Garlic(with whom he did a couple of John Peel sessions, attended SXSW, released 2 albums with Bella Union label, and supported New Order at the Brixton Academy).

Huw Evans

Guitar

Hack fraud guitarist specialising in aimless fret-wankery. After picking up a guitar during the gestative years of noughties indie rock landfill, the fretting fingers were honed in school mates myspace bands in Cardiff. Fled the UK completely for a few years because of the shitty public transport, migrating to Japan play metal/punk rock with the radioactive wild boars of Fukushima. Radioactive wild boars is not the name of a band as there were literally wild boars roaming the quarantined area near the Fukushima Daiichi nuclear plant. Although Radioactive Wild Boars isn't a bad name for a band. Finally returned to the UK, moving to ye olde Londone to make a racket across town with the Strip n Roll fellas. Come to our shows and you might learn a thing or two about your life. Probably not. But you're not going to learn anything by staying at home and watching reruns of Countdown. Strip n Roll shows are more fun than Countdown anyway. Maybe not as fun as Blue Planet 2. RIP Richard Whitely. Peace be with you. Eat your vegetables!

Nick Simms

Drums/Backing Vocals

After being kicked out of guitar lessons at school because of persistent lateness, Nick discovered his timekeeping skills behind the drum kit. From The Septembers and via Dubh Chapter he found himself in Cornershop. Later still, he spent time behind the kit with Natacha Atlas, Viarosa and Johnny Boy, amongst others.

Felipe Goncalves

Bass

Began playing keyboards and electric guitar, listening to bands like Dire Straits, Pink Floyd, The Cure... In late 80’s got into to metal bands like Iron Maiden, Metallica, Megadeth, Guns n’ Roses, AC/DC... In the late 90’s, he started playing in small clubs in Porto. Over the years, he's been writing and playing in many bands, also doing lots of home instrumental music recordings. Moved to London in 2014 and joined Strip n Roll.
